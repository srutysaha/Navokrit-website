ABOUT US

Welcome to Navokrit, an AIML (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) club dedicated to empowering individuals with job-ready skills through immersive, hands-on workshops. We are a passionate community of AI enthusiasts who believe in the transformative power of AIML technologies. At Navokrit, our primary focus is on practical learning experiences. We believe that the best way to develop job-ready skills is through active engagement and real-world application. Our workshops are designed to provide members with the opportunity to work on industry-relevant projects, solve complex problems, and gain invaluable hands-on experience.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation in the field of Information Technology. We aim to equip our members with the skills and expertise required to thrive in the rapidly evolving AI-driven job market. By fostering a collaborative and supportive learning environment, we strive to cultivate a community of individuals who are well-prepared to tackle the challenges of the future.Through our workshops, we aim to demystify AI and machine learning as well as other growing technological concepts, making them accessible to individuals from diverse backgrounds. We believe in nurturing a culture of continuous learning, where our members can expand their knowledge, explore new ideas, and push the boundaries of innovation.
